Sarah Alnegheimish

I am an SM student at MIT in the Center of Computational Science and Engineering (CCSE), advised by Dr. Kalyan Veeramachaneni, and a member of the Data to AI Lab at LIDS. My research interest is in the field of Applied Machine Learning and Data Science. I work on applying deep learning to solve various challenges, particularly within the context of time series. I am also interested in building systems that democratize the usage of machine learning.
