Sarah Alnegheimish

I am an S.M. student at MIT pursing a dual degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) and Computational Science and Engineering (CCSE), advised by Dr. Kalyan Veeramachaneni, and a member of the Data to AI Lab at LIDS. My research interest is in the field of Applied Machine Learning and Data Science. I work on applying deep learning to solve various challenges, particularly within the context of time series. I am also interested in open-source development and in building systems that make machine learning usable.
